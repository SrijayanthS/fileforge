About

File tools that stay on your device

FileForge helps you resize images, convert image formats, merge PDFs, split PDFs by page range, and turn images into PDFs without sending your files to a backend service.

Help / FAQ

Common questions

Which image files can I use?

FileForge supports JPG, PNG, and WebP images for upload, preview, resizing, conversion, and image-to-PDF export.

How do I split a PDF?

Upload a PDF, enter a range such as 1-3 or 2,4,6, then select Split PDF. The first uploaded PDF is used for splitting.

Why are some buttons disabled?

Actions become available when the right file is loaded. For example, merge needs at least two PDFs, and download appears after processing finishes.

Privacy

Private by design

Your files are processed locally in your browser. FileForge does not upload images or PDFs to a server, and there is no backend receiving your documents.
